# ShopVac Rat Trap - Assembly Guide

> [!NOTE]
> **Tool-Free Assembly**: No screws or screwdrivers required for the main body. Parts snap and twist together.

## 1. Module Overview
![Exploded View](/home/bandwith/ShopVacRatTrap/images/assembly_exploded.svg)
*Identify your printed parts: Entrance, Sensor Module, Bait Station, Control Box.*

## 2. Connect Modules (Twist-Lock)
![Twist-Lock Mechanism](/home/bandwith/ShopVacRatTrap/images/assembly_twist_lock.svg)
1. Align the **dots** on the tubes.
2. Push together.
3. **Twist** clockwise until it clicks.

## 3. Route Cables
![Cable Routing](/home/bandwith/ShopVacRatTrap/images/assembly_cables.svg)
1. Feed sensor wires into the **top channel**.
2. Ensure connectors pass all the way through to the Control Box end.

## 4. Install Sensors
![Sensor Installation](/home/bandwith/ShopVacRatTrap/images/assembly_sensors.svg)
1. Press sensors into their mounts.
2. **Snap** the covers on to secure them.

## 5. Attach Control Box
![Control Box Assembly](/home/bandwith/ShopVacRatTrap/images/assembly_control_box.svg)
1. Plug cables into the ESP32.
2. Snap the Control Box onto the rear rail.
3. Slide the lid closed.

## 6. Bait & Arm
![Bait Cap](/home/bandwith/ShopVacRatTrap/images/assembly_bait.svg)
1. Unscrew the Bait Cap.
2. Add peanut butter.
3. Screw cap back on tight.
